Weekly Outline for Productive Tasks

Reddit Community Building (8 hours)

Content Creation (4 hours)

  • Draft 3-4 detailed posts for relevant subreddits. This could include product updates, useful tutorials, engaging discussions, or even AMA (Ask Me Anything) sessions to introduce our brand to the community.
  • Design or source accompanying graphics or GIFs for each post.

Engagement (3 hours)

  • Actively participate in related subreddit threads, answer questions, and provide valuable insights.
  • Respond to comments on our posts, upvote relevant comments, and engage genuinely to foster trust.

Research (1 hour)

  • Identify popular and relevant subreddits where our target audience might be active.
  • Observe trending topics in these subreddits for content ideas and opportunities to introduce our brand.

Twitter Management (7 hours)

Content Creation (3 hours)

  • Draft and schedule 10 tweets highlighting key product features, updates, or relevant industry news.
  • Create 2 engaging Twitter polls or questions.

Engagement & Community Building (2 hours)

  • Respond to mentions, comments, and direct messages.
  • Engage with industry influencers by liking, retweeting, and commenting.

Analysis & Strategy (2 hours)

  • Analyze tweet performance using Twitter Analytics.
  • Strategize for upcoming tweets based on insights.

Developer Relations & Feedback (3 hours)

Reddit (1.5 hours)

  • Initiate discussions related to development on our subreddit.
  • Engage in developer-focused subreddits, sharing tips and tricks.

Twitter (1.5 hours)

  • Engage with developers, share developer-centric content, and spotlight user-generated content.
  • Collect feedback and address any developer-centric issues.

Strategy & Planning (2 hours)

  • Reflect on the week's performance, both on Twitter and Reddit.
  • Plan for the next week, adjusting strategies based on feedback and observations.
